# Official UN SDG icons

These are the **official United Nations Sustainable Development Goals icons**
(`E-WEB-Goal-01.png` … `E-WEB-Goal-17.png`), used **unmodified**.

- **Source:** UN SDG communications material —
  https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/news/communications-material/
  (web icon package `E-SDG-Icons-WEB.zip`, downloaded 2026-06).
- **Guidelines:** “Guidelines for the use of the SDG logo and the 17 SDG icons” —
  https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/wp-content/uploads/2023/09/E_SDG_Guidelines_Sep20238.pdf

## Usage terms (why this is allowed here)

Third parties may use the SDG icons for **informational / awareness purposes
without prior permission**. The ZOE platform is a non-commercial municipal /
academic information platform — a permitted use. Conditions we comply with:

- **No modification** — icons are used exactly as distributed (no recolouring,
  cropping or merging into other logos).
- **Non-commercial** — no advertising, no fundraising, no product promotion.
- **Attribution + disclaimer** are shown in the UI on the SDG dashboard
  (link to https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment and the UN disclaimer:
  “The content of this publication has not been approved by the United Nations
  and does not reflect the views of the United Nations or its officials or
  Member States.”).

If usage ever becomes commercial/fundraising, written permission is required
via https://shop.un.org/form/sdg-request-form.
